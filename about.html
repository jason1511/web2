About this archive

This site is a personal archive of photographs and in-game screenshots. It exists primarily as a place to collect, catalogue, and view images — not as a social feed or blog.

What you’ll find here

  • Real-world photos taken casually over time
  • Screenshots from video games and simulations
  • Minimal editing, minimal curation
  • No algorithms, no engagement chasing

How it’s organised

Images are grouped and filterable by year, source, and type. Metadata is kept intentionally simple so the focus stays on the image itself.

How it’s built

This site is intentionally lightweight. Images are stored in object storage and served directly, while the gallery and catalogue are rendered client-side. Uploads and updates are handled through a private admin interface.

No trackers. No analytics. No social widgets.
